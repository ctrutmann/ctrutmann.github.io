Seasons in the Country

Each season is announced by its own heralds and has its own charm. (Except the season I have dubbed Thaw, which they left out of my kindergarten education, and which I have also left out of this website, because I'm still struggling to find anything charming in a lot of mud).

A very pretty (and somewhat related) website on seasonal country living is Creative Country, which I only just discovered in the process of creating this page. It has a very clean, minimalist design and layout with gorgeous photography and thoughtful content.
